Official Rules
1. 24-hour advance league. Mon-Thursday at midnight CST. 36 Hour advance on weekends Advance on weekends will be at 12 noon on Saturday and Midnight on Sunday all times are CST. All users must create a coach when they join.
2.Scheduling

a. Must reach out to opponent via text/dm to schedule game by 3 pm CST. Chat messages will not count as communication has to be in text/DM for force win. Chat will only count if there is a clear cut agreement with time and confirmation

b. If no response by 3 pm, Non-respondent team can be placed on auto at 3:01 pm CST

c. If neither person response to each other or a commissioner by advance the game will be a 50/50 sim

d. if one party does not show by the agreed time he has 30 minutes to show or the other party will get the force.

3. Position Changes

a. CB to FS or SS vice versa is okay

b. LB to DE vice versa is okay

c. No OL or WR to TE unless formation sub

d. WR to RB must be 6’3 or shorter

e. RB/WR/TE to FB must be 6’3 or shorter and weigh 235 or higher

f. RB/WR/FB to TE must be 6’3 or taller and weigh 235 lbs or higher

g. DB to LB must be 6 foot or taller and weigh 225 Lbs or higher

4. Position Change Manipulation rule week to week for XP points

a. You can only change a guys position once a season

b. no changes after week 10 unless approved by A commissioner

5. Game vs CPU is allowed if your opponent is on auto

a. you have 1 try vs CPU its ok to quit vs CPU but the game will become a 50/50 sim

b. TD rules vs CPU 4TDs RB 4 TDs WR 5 TDs QB
